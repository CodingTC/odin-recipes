Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Pizza Dough

Steps

  1. Mold the dough in a heart shape
  2. Spread your marinara across the dough
  3. Put the mozzarella over the marinara
  4. Apply the pepperoni on the pizza
  5. Put in the oven at 375 for ten minutes
  6. Let it rest for 2 minutes then enjoy
